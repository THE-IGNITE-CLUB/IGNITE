About IGNITE

🔥 Established March 04, 2010

IGNITE is the premier student leadership and innovation club of SVUCE, Tirupati. Founded on the spirit of transforming student potential into real-world impact, IGNITE has been the breeding ground for visionary leaders, creative thinkers, and change-makers.

Our distinguished alumni — Suresh Perugu and Dilli Babu — stand as enduring testaments to the club's legacy of excellence and its capacity to shape future leaders.

Our Roots

About SVUCE & SVU

Sri Venkateswara University College of Engineering (SVUCE)

Established in 1959, SVUCE is one of Andhra Pradesh's oldest and most prestigious engineering colleges, situated on the sprawling campus of Sri Venkateswara University in Tirupati. Over six decades, SVUCE has nurtured thousands of engineers who have made significant contributions to industry, academia, and public service across India and the globe.

Sri Venkateswara University (SVU)

Sri Venkateswara University was established in 1954 in Tirupati, Andhra Pradesh. Named after the presiding deity of the Tirumala Hills, the university has grown into a comprehensive centre of higher learning with multiple faculties covering Science, Arts, Commerce, Engineering, Law, and more. It has been consistently recognised for its academic excellence and research contributions.
